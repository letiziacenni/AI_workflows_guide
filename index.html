AI & Data

Inferring Sensitive Data from Public Information

AI models can use university acronyms and names to infer country of origin, gender, title, and cultural norms — without direct access to sensitive fields.

Act as a data analyst. You will receive an Excel file with a student list. Create new columns for: country of origin (from university acronym), gender (from student name + country), and title (from gender). Return the enriched table.
⚠️
Ethics disclosure AI-inferred sensitive data mirrors how corporations use big data to deduce social status, purchasing power, and personal details from general data. This opens significant ethical concerns around privacy and data protection.

Knowledge Management

PARA Method with ChatGPT + Obsidian

Use ChatGPT to organise your notes into the PARA structure (Projects, Areas, Resources, Archives), then store them locally in Obsidian for a safe, private, local-first setup.

Act as a knowledge management assistant. Organise my notes using the PARA method. Categorise each item into the right folder, suggest clear names, and flag anything for Archive (inactive items). Keep the structure simple and easy to maintain.
🛡️
Ethics disclosure Obsidian's local-first approach keeps your data on your device by default. However, syncing or collaborating via cloud means your data may be stored elsewhere — stay mindful of access rights.

Productivity

AI as Personal Assistant — Calendar Integration with Claude

Claude.ai can read calendar entries (titles, times, agenda links), identify actionable items and deadlines, suggest readings before meetings, and propose "focus time" blocks.

Act as a productivity assistant. I'll give you access to my calendar. Identify actionable items and deadlines. Suggest a daily to-do list, highlight pre-meeting readings, and propose focus time blocks. Keep it concise and practical.
⚠️
Ethics disclosure Calendar AI access can expose sensitive meeting data and internal documents — especially via "shadow AI" tools not approved by corporate IT. The Irish HSE breach is a cautionary example. Only use company-approved tools.
Claude.aiCalendar

Content Strategy

Quantitative + Qualitative Analysis with Perplexity AI

Combine performance data (clicks, views) with audience feedback (comments, surveys) to generate insights on what content works and why — with actionable content ideas.

Act as a content and media strategist. I'll give you quantitative data (pageviews, CTR) and qualitative data (comments, surveys). Analyse both and produce insights on audience needs, then suggest 3–5 content domains and creative concepts.
⚖️
Ethics disclosure AI may over-recommend "feel-good" high-engagement topics while ignoring important low-performing ones. Counter this: for every 3 high-performing topics, force 1 "low-data but high-value" topic, and check who is missing from your data.
Perplexity AI

Prompt Engineering

Use ChatGPT to Build Better Prompts for Notion AI

Use one AI to optimise your instructions before passing them to another. This "prompt chaining" approach produces more structured, precise results in Notion AI.

Act as a prompt engineer. Rewrite my rough instruction into a clear, structured prompt optimised for Notion AI. Include: the AI's role, the task, the expected format, and constraints (tone, length, style). [Add rough instructions + ideal success result].
🔁
Ethics disclosure A sunk-cost fallacy can set in: once you've optimised prompts, you keep using them even if they no longer serve your original goal. Periodically step back and check whether the workflow still aligns with your intentions.

Content Creation

Claude for Content Generation and Cross-Publishing

Claude.ai can turn a rough idea into a short, engaging social post — with an image suggestion — ready for platforms like Bluesky. Great for small teams with limited content resources.

Act as a social media editor. [Add context, e.g. small regional university]. I'll give you a rough post idea. Turn it into a short, engaging Bluesky post (max ~300 characters) in clear, natural language. Suggest one simple image idea. Keep the tone informative and accessible.
🧠
Ethics disclosure Claude uses Constitutional AI — trained on Anthropic's detailed values. Even so, AI results can still be incomplete or imperfect. Human review is always needed before publishing.

Regulatory Analysis

Understand Complex Regulations with NotebookLM (EU AI Act Example)

Upload official legal documents into NotebookLM and let AI produce overviews, summaries, and adaptive takeaways — with full citations back to the source material. Ideal for complex regulatory texts like the EU AI Act.

Act as a policy analyst. Using only the provided EU AI Act documents, create: (1) a plain-language overview, (2) a breakdown of risk categories, (3) practical implications for organisations + "what to do now" summary, (4) key definitions and compliance requirements. Include source citations throughout.
✅
Ethics disclosure NotebookLM grounds all responses in user-provided sources, reducing hallucinations and improving traceability. For complex regulation like the EU AI Act, this source-linked approach significantly improves reliability over general AI queries.

Information Retrieval

Gemini + Google Drive for Briefing Preparation

Gemini can scan your entire Google Drive and pull relevant information from across notes, slides, and emails — perfect for preparing a briefing without manual searching.

Act as a research assistant. Search my Google Drive for anything related to "[topic]". Identify relevant documents, extract key decisions and deadlines, highlight contradictions, and summarise everything into max 5 bullets. Reference source file and date for each point.
💡
Ethics disclosure Relying on one ecosystem increases lock-in and data exposure. Tip: create a dedicated "safe-to-scan" folder (e.g. Briefings_AI) and only run AI queries there. Avoid pointing Gemini at your entire Drive by default.

SEO & Podcasting

AI-Driven Podcast Topic Generation with SEO Data

Use search data and audience questions to surface what people are genuinely curious or confused about, then turn those insights into podcast episode ideas framed as real questions.

Act as a podcast editor and SEO analyst. [Add context]. You'll receive keyword data, audience questions, and related search data. Group queries into topics and turn them into episode ideas framed as real questions (e.g. "How do I apply for…?"). Suggest a clear title and short description for each.
⚖️
Ethics disclosure SEO-driven AI can overrepresent popular groups and push engaging topics over complex, important ones. Mitigation: use only anonymised, aggregated data — and keep humans in the review loop for all topic decisions.

Personal Productivity

OCR Text Scanner + Fabric.so as an AI Repository

As you scroll LinkedIn or other feeds, an OCR scanner captures actionable items from your screen. AI then filters for relevant tasks and routes them into Fabric.so, Instapaper, or Readwise for review.

No single prompt — this is a workflow: OCR captures screen text → AI filters for only actionable items → cleaned results are sorted into folders ("To do", "Read later", "Ideas") → human review step before final storage.
🔒
Ethics disclosure Screen-scanning OCR tools may capture unintended sensitive data. The Yahoo data breach (2024) is a reminder: the more you capture, the more you expose. Always include a human review step before storage. The more you capture, the bigger the target.
